Game On!

Get set for Toe-to-Toe Tic-Tac-Toe! A classic game of wits where Xs and Os clash in a fun-filled grid. Gather your pals, choose your symbol, and jump into the tic-tac-tussle. Ready for the challenge? Let's see who conquers the grid!

This is how you play!

  • The gameboard grid is initially empty.
  • You are player that uses "X" as your mark, and the computer uses "O."
  • Players take turns, starting with the "X" player.
  • Each cell can only be selected once.
  • One player gets three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). This player wins the game.
  • The entire grid is filled with marks, and no player has three in a row. This results in a draw (a "cat's game").

Click "Reset" below the grid to restart.
